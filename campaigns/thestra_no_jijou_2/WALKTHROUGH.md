# Whispers Beneath Caer Vorn -- Walkthrough

## Start
The player begins in **Caer Vorn** (town hub). Speak with **Warden Hella** at the town center. She gives three quests: `investigate_well`, `recover_sealed_page`, and `end_the_cultist`.

## Act I: The Descent Begins
1. Accept `investigate_well` from Warden Hella.
2. Leave town and enter **Upper Catacombs** (depth 2).
3. Fight **Husk Hound** packs (creatures die permanently, convert to banked EXP).
4. Reach the flooded junction and find the broken wardstone (Act I climax).
5. Return to Caer Vorn if desired; Hella confirms the page is needed below.

## Act II: Roots of the Rot
1. Accept `recover_sealed_page`. Enter **Sunken Library** (depth 3).
2. Defeat the **Cursed Scholar** debuffer spirit to obtain the Sealed Page.
3. Cross **Bone Gallery** (depth 4), mind collapsing floors, then descend to **Hollow Sanctum** (depth 5).
4. Accept/complete `end_the_cultist` by fighting the **Parasite Cultist** boss in Hollow Sanctum (Act II climax).
5. With the cultist dead, use the Sealed Page to seal the sanctum.

## Ending
The Summoner exits to Caer Vorn. Banked EXP from lost creatures is kept. The well runs clear; the campaign concludes.
