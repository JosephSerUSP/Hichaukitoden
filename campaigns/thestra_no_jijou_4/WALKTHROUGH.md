# Thestra no Jijou: The Curse of the Sunken Temple -- Walkthrough

## Overview
This is a compact 30–45 minute campaign. You play as a Summoner who captures monsters (creatures) to fight in a first-person dungeon crawler. Creatures die permanently and convert to banked EXP, so manage your team carefully. The campaign consists of one town map and four dungeon floors. Follow the critical path below.

---

## Act 1: The Blight Appears

1. **Start in Thestra Village** – You arrive at the town. The sky is dark with storm clouds. The village has three key NPCs: Elder Aris (in the large central building), Healer Mina (in her hut, but she is not there yet – she has gone missing), and Shopkeeper Garrick (at the market stall).

2. **Talk to Elder Aris** – He explains that a blight has been spreading from the Sunken Temple, making livestock sick and crops wither. He gives you the quest **"The Temple Blight"** (main quest). He also mentions that Healer Mina went into the temple to help and hasn't returned. This triggers the side quest **"Missing Healer"** (you can accept it from him or from a villager – the game marks it as active).

3. **Prepare at the shop** – Buy basic healing items and perhaps a monster capture orb from Garrick. He also sells a cheap sword and armor if you need. You start with a basic summonable creature (e.g., a Wind Sprite).

4. **Enter the Sunken Temple Floor 1: Hall of Echoes** – The entrance is behind the elder's house. On Floor 1, explore the linear corridors. You'll encounter weak spirits (element: Shadow) and crawling insects (element: Green). Use your starting creature to fight. You can also capture new monsters (e.g., a Stone Crab with Tank role) if you have capture orbs. Be careful: traps (spikes, gas vents) can weaken your party.

5. **Find Healer Mina** – On Floor 1, look for a side passage with a locked door (the key is from a chest in the main path). Behind it, Mina is hurt and surrounded by small blight creatures. Defeat them and talk to her. She thanks you and tells you that the blight originates from deeper levels, and that three ancient tokens are needed for a purification ritual. She asks you to escort her back to the village. The quest **"Missing Healer"** completes. She gives you a reward (e.g., a Soothing Mote skill scroll).

6. **Return to town** – Report to Elder Aris. He is relieved and suggests you help Mina with the tokens. Talk to Healer Mina again in her hut to receive the next quest **"The Three Tokens"**. This begins Act 2.

---

## Act 2: The Three Tokens

1. **Quest: The Three Tokens** – You need to collect three tokens: Earth (from Floor 2), Fire (from Floor 3), and Shadow (from Floor 4). They can be acquired in any order, but the recommended order is Earth → Fire → Shadow.

2. **Sunken Temple Floor 2: Vault of Decay** – Descend from Floor 1. This floor is darker and full of poison pools. Equip a creature with Green resistance if possible. The boss here is a decayed golem (element: Green). It uses poison and weaken states. Defeat it to obtain the Token of Earth. Also look for a healing spring room that restores HP once.

3. **Sunken Temple Floor 3: Chamber of Sacrifice** – From the end of Floor 2, proceed to Floor 3. This is a large chamber with fire traps. The boss is the Guardian Golem (role: Tank, element: Red). It is immune to physical attacks until you hit its core with a magic skill. Use a Caster or Support creature with a spell like Wind Blade. After victory, collect the Token of Fire from the altar. There is also a checkpoint save point here.

4. **Return to town to restock** – You can go back to Thestra Village between floors. Healer Mina can heal your creatures for free (thanks to her gratitude). Shopkeeper Garrick restocks items. You may want to capture new creatures from the dungeon to replace any losses.

5. **Sunken Temple Floor 4: Sanctum of the Fallen** – The deepest floor. It is a linear path with tough shadow spirits. At the far end is a pedestal with the Token of Shadow, guarded by a powerful cursed spirit (boss-level but not the final boss). It uses Sleep and Debuff abilities. Beat it and take the token. Now you have all three.

---

## Act 3: Purification

1. **Return to Healer Mina** – Bring the three tokens to her hut. She performs a ritual and creates a Purification Stone. She also tells you that the source of the blight is a being called the Cursebeast in the inner sanctum on Floor 4 (a room beyond where you found the token). She warns that it is incredibly strong and uses Shadow and berserk states.

2. **Final Dungeon trek** – Go back to Floor 4. From the token room, proceed to a previously locked door (the key is now available since you have the Purification Stone). Enter the Sanctum of the Fallen's inner chamber.

3. **Boss: Cursebeast** – This is the final boss. It is a large amorphous monster (element: Shadow, role: Assassin/Vampire hybrid). It has the following abilities: Shadow Claw (high damage), Drain Kiss (heals itself), and can apply Poison and Weaken states. It also has a passive: it sometimes revives a defeated ally? No, that's not in the kit. But it can summon two small shadow adds at half health. Strategy: bring a Healer creature (or use Field Surgery on yourself), use a Tank to soak damage, and an Attacker to focus on the main body. The adds are weak but annoying. Use items to remove poison. When it's low, it may use berserk on itself – defend through that.

4. **Victory and ritual** – After defeating the Cursebeast, use the Purification Stone on the dark crystal at the back of the room. The blight recedes, the ground trembles, and light floods in. A cutscene shows the storm clearing above Thestra.

5. **Ending** – You return to the village as a celebration begins. Elder Aris thanks you. Healer Mina offers to join you as a support NPC (optional, not controllable). All your surviving summoned creatures gain bonus EXP from the banked pool. The campaign ends with the message: "The curse is broken. Thestra breathes again."

---

## Tips
- **Permadeath** – If a creature dies in combat, it is permanently lost but grants a chunk of EXP to your global bank. Use this to level up new captures. Don't get attached to any single creature until late game.
- **Elemental weaknesses** – Check the element of enemies. For example, Shadow is weak to White, Red is weak to Blue, Green is weak to Red, etc. Exploit them.
- **Use Wait** – Sometimes skipping a turn (Wait) is better than attacking into a defensive enemy.
- **Save often** – There are no auto-saves in this engine? The walkthrough assumes the engine has a save function at town and at some checkpoints.

This walkthrough covers the critical path and should be completable in about 35 minutes with moderate difficulty. Good luck, Summoner!
