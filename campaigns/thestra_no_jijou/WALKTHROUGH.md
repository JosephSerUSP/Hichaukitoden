# Thestra: Ash Below -- Walkthrough

## Start
Player begins in **Thestra Hub** (town). Speak with **Engineer Voss** to accept **clear_lower_levels** and later **restore_purifier**. Speak with **Healer Mira** to accept **aid_survivors**.

## Act 1: Descent Prep
From Thestra Hub, enter **Thestra Intake** (depth 1 dungeon). Fight minor corrupted nodes to learn summon mechanics. At the end of the intake, descend to **Corroded Conduit**.

## Act 2: Clearing the Depths
In **Corroded Conduit**, fight and defeat **Husk Brute** (creature). Move to **Pulse Reactor**, defeat **Static Wraith** (creature) to restore power. In **Root Cavity**, defeat **Thorn Parasite** (creature) to open the path. Descend to **Hydro Core** and fight Boss **Ash Sovereign**. Upon victory, the purifier activates.

## Act 3: Restoration
Return to **Thestra Hub**. Report to **Engineer Voss** and **Healer Mira**. The campaign concludes with Thestra's life support stabilized and the Summoner honored.
